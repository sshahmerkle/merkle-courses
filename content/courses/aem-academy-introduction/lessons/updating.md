AEM is not only for new page creation - it is also used for ongoing content management.

When updating existing pages:

- Confirm whether the page is live
- Assess downstream impact (linked pages, navigation, campaigns)
- Use version history where appropriate
- Avoid deleting assets that may be used elsewhere

Content should always be updated responsibly, especially on high-traffic pages.
