AEM Sites enables us to build structured, scalable, and brand-consistent digital experiences.

As an author, your key responsibilities are:

- Use templates correctly
- Select appropriate components
- Manage assets responsibly
- Follow governance and workflow processes
- Conduct pre-flight and QA checks per page/ brief to help mitigate against potential issues later down the line.

The system provides structure. Your role is to bring the content to life within that structure.
