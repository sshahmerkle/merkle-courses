Once Formal QA checks have been completed the page can now be published. However for the purpose of this training, we will not publish the page. However you will find this in the below settings:

1. Navigate to **Page Information** in the top toolbar (second to left image of the Dial)
2. Select **Publish Page**.

Depending on your permissions, the page will either:

- Publish directly to the live environment, or
- Be submitted to an **approval workflow** for review.

Once approved and published, the page will become available on the live website.
