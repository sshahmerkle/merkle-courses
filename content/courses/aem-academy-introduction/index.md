## Overview

Adobe Experience Manager (AEM) is a powerful, enterprise‑level content management system designed to help organisations build, manage, and deliver consistent digital experiences across websites, apps, and other customer touchpoints.

This course introduces the core concepts of AEM, including how content is created and structured, how components and templates shape page design, and how AEM Sites and Assets work together to support modern content workflows.

By the end of the course, learners will understand the fundamentals of authoring, organising, and publishing content in AEM, giving them the foundation needed to confidently work within the platform.

## What You Will Learn

- How AEM works as a content management system and the key concepts behind Sites, Assets, and the content repository
- How to create, edit, and publish content using AEM’s authoring tools
- The role of templates and components in building page structure and reusable content
- How digital assets are managed, organised, and delivered using AEM Assets
- Best‑practice workflows for collaborating, approving, and publishing content in AEM
